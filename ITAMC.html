What is IT AMC?

Annual Maintenance Contract — One Agreement That Covers Everything

An IT AMC (Annual Maintenance Contract) is a service agreement where Chipin Corp takes full responsibility for maintaining, monitoring, and supporting your entire IT infrastructure for a fixed annual or monthly fee.

Instead of calling for help only when things break — and paying emergency rates — your AMC gives you a dedicated team that proactively keeps everything running smoothly, all year round.

Covers all hardware & software

Desktops, laptops, servers, printers, network devices, switches, and all business software.

Includes on-site and remote support

Our engineers come to your Dubai office — or resolve issues remotely — within guaranteed response times.

No hidden costs

One flat monthly fee covers all standard support — no surprise invoices for routine work.
